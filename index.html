Fabio Furlotti

Between street and portrait, I wait. Emotion becomes story.

Fabio Furlotti (born 1973, Parma) is an Italian photographer working exclusively in black and white. His practice spans portrait and street photography, with a visual language rooted in the tradition of Peter Lindbergh and the night photography of Helmut Newton.


His portrait subjects include Academy Award and Palme d'Or-winning directors, international actors, and fashion icons. His photographs appear on the covers of books published by Bompiani and Liguori Editore.


In 2016, he won the C/O Berlin "Urbane Kontraste" international photography contest, selected from 40,000 entries. He shoots with Leica M11 Monochrom and is Certified by Leica (CBL).
