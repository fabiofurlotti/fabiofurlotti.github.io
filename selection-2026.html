STATEMENT — COPIA NEL CAMPO "DESCRIZIONE SERIE" DEL FORM AIRTABLE (min 150 parole · solo EN)
"Fragments of Presence" brings together eight photographs made in public and urban space — a street corner, a city square, a bus, a station, a night street — in Parma and Bologna, Italy.

The series is built around one recurring condition: the way human presence registers in ordinary places without asking for attention. A barista framed by a corner in Bologna. A couple caught kissing in the fog. A woman on a bus, looking inward. A man walking a dog through lamplight. Fog erasing the train tracks.

The images were not arranged or directed. They were found. Working in black and white, the sequence relies on atmosphere, tonal restraint, and spatial compression rather than on event or confrontation. The people are present, but not performing. The spaces hold them without comment.

This is less about decisive moments than about the residue left when nothing decisive happens — when the city and the people in it simply coexist, briefly and without explanation.

Fabio Furlotti is an Italian photographer based in Parma. He works between street photography and portraiture, primarily in black and white.

Corpus / Destinazione

Outside Performance
Portraits of artists and cultural figures photographed outside the moment of performance. Working in black and white, Furlotti stays close to faces, pauses, and unstable intervals in which the subject is still visible as a public figure, but no longer fully protected by that position.
Destinazione: Eyeshot (#1) · GUP · y Magazine  ·  Stato: corpus costruito, pagina sito da fare

Scegliere le immagini in cui la persona pubblica sembra meno protetta dal proprio ruolo.
Formato didascalie: Name, Place, Year — niente professione, niente caption narrative.
